About Us

The Coffee House is an exciting new cafe expected to open october 2021 in Marietta, Ohio. Our goal is to brew the finestest quality coffee, whilst maintaining sustainability, and using free trade coffee beans. We want to do out part, and for every cup of coffee served, we will donate 5% back to support the farms most in need.
